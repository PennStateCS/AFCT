# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

This is a multi-institution academic collaboration. We want participation in the
project to be a harassment-free, respectful experience for everyone, regardless of
background, identity, or level of experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Assume good faith and welcome newcomers.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and sharing others' private
information without consent are not tolerated in any project space.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainer at **jdc308@psu.edu**.
Reports will be handled discreetly and reviewed promptly.

This code of conduct is adapted in spirit from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
